Prose and Pros

Brenden Legros

Along with the rapid development of cybernetic technology in recent years, artificial intelligence has been seen almost in all our life circles. However, there hasn't been much discussion in the realm of social sciences. AI will have a wide-ranging impact on various aspects of society, including arts, and cultural and creative industries as well as mental health, be it positive or negative.

Human well-being, both physical and digital, is being called into question by the technological revolution with artificial intelligence. The utilization of quantitative data has recently piqued the interest of a wide range of domains. Does the data, as well as the biases contained within it, influence decisions that have a direct impact on people's well-being? Do we need to take those biases into consideration when analyzing the data? Is it possible to improve our ability to understand complex human behavior by applying AI technology to existing data? How can artificial intelligence (AI) be used to improve people's lives?

This panel discussion aims to answer these questions and determine how artificial intelligence can be used to ensure the mental health of people. This multifaceted discussion will help you understand the impact of AI on the well-being of humans and give you a philosophical perspective of the same. Well, we promise you that this won't be one of those boring lectures, because students like you will also be a part of this panel to put forth your point.
